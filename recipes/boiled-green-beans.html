Congee

Description

With a super easy method and perfectly tender results, this easy side dish can be on the table in 10 minutes to share with the whole family!

This super simple green bean recipe is our go-to since it’s lightning fast and is consistently so delicious. It’s also a great texture for kids and adults and can be paired with a whole range of types of meals.

Ingredients

  • 10-16 ounces green beans
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter or olive oil
  • Salt
  • 0.50 oz ginger

Steps

  1. Wash your green beans. Break off the ends and any brown parts. Break in half if very long.
  2. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil over high heat, then reduce to a simmer over medium-high heat. Add the beans and simmer for 4-6 minutes or until tender when poked with a fork.
  3. Drain the water from the beans, then add butter or olive oil, and season to taste with salt. (I usually just add a little sprinkle.)
